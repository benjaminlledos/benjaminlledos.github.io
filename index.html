Presentation

Welcome to my webpage. My research areas are the calculus of variations, elliptic PDEs and geometric measure theory. I am interested in the uniqueness, regularity, and qualitative properties of solutions to minimization problems in the calculus of variations or elliptic PDEs.

Since September 2024, I am a maître de conférences (associate professor) at the université de Nîmes, working in the Laboratoire mathématiques, informatique, physique et applications (MIPA). Previously, I worked in the Institut de recherche en mathématique et physique (IRMP) of the UC Louvain as a Postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Heiner Olbermann.

My Ph.D. was defended in July 2023 and supervised by Pierre Bousquet at the Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse.

This webpage gathers information and documents about my research and teaching activities as well as my educational background.
